About me

DJ and producer HI ROSIE, spotted by Laurent Garnier and gboi, already endorsed by major artists of the world scene such as VTSS, Tzusing and Sinjin Hawke, produces music rooted in furious, contemporary bass music. He's finally out of the bedroom. Under the aegis of a minimalism where it is not a question of adding but rather of removing to finish, his truth lies in a rejection of overproduction and a race to the essential. A quest for the simple and the effective. Between raw FM synthesis, lyrical vocals, romantic orchestral instruments and deconstructed rhythms, the artist offers us two distinct aesthetics in his music: one where the energy of the club and the dance is at the centre, and the other, much more sensitive and romantic.
